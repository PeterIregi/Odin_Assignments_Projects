Peanut Butter Banana Smothie

This peanut butter banana smoothie is so refreshing, and it's sweet and tasty. Prep Time: 5 mins Total Time: 5 mins Servings: 4 Ingredients 1/2x 1x 2x Original recipe (1X) yields 4 servings 2 bananas, broken into chunks 2 cups milk ½ cup peanut butter 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste 2 cups ice cubes Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie. How to Freeze Bananas for Banana Smoothies Though the recipe itself doesn't call for frozen bananas, many reviewers recommend it. Freezing bananas is a great way to preserve them for future smoothies. Here's an easy way to freeze bananas for smoothies: Peel the bananas and cut them into 1-inch pieces. Arrange the banana slices on a baking sheet and slide the sheet into the freezer. When the pieces have frozen, transfer them into a resealable freezer bag and store in the freezer until smoothie time! Directions Gather all ingredients. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds. Enjoy!
